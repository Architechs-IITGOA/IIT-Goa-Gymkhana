ABOUT US

Greetings from IIT Goa's Culturals! At IIT Goa we undertake a variety of cultural activities, which are broadly classified under 6 genres – Dance, Music, Dramatics, Fine Arts, Literary, and Photography.

Culturals @IIT Goa promotes a vibrant and electic campus experience through organizing innumerable multifarious activities pertaining to the cultural events, bringing people from all walks, interests, and ethnicity come together in ways to stimulate community interaction.

All starts from the freshers party ‘Fresco Fiesta’ welcoming the new entrants to the new family, to Independence Day and Republic Day, from Eid to Diwali, Christmas to Lohri, Teacher’s Day to Children’s Day, and the most exciting ‘CultRang’. IIT Goa’s cultural council, with a blend of Goan culture presents extravaganza of art and culture, entertaining and enriching the lives of the people at the institute.

In photography there is a reality so subtle that it becomes more real than reality.” Originals, the photography club of IIT Goa, being one of the most active clubs go IIT Goa, encourages amateur photographers and beginners interested in learning the techniques of photography by providing them with all the latest equipment ranging from professional cameras, tripods to camcorders, etc.

Qalam

Description

Every secret of a writer's soul, every experience of his life, every quality of his mind, is written large in his works.” Qalam, the literary club of IIT Goa, aims at developing and honing the literary skills of the students. The club inspires students to develop a taste for literature and also works in the direction of expanding their horizon of spoken and written language. The club is based upon using the creativity skills it takes to be a writer.

Club Head

Meraki

Description

To dance is to be out of yourself. Larger, more beautiful, more powerful… This is power, it is glory on earth and it is yours for the taking” Meraki, the dance club of IIT Goa, is one of the most glamorous and hardworking clubs. Mukhota

All the world's a stage and most of us are desperately unrehearsed. “ Mukhota, Dramatics club of IIT Goa, is one of the oldest and most talented clubs. From writing plays, to direction and to perform, the members get to learn each of the skills mentioned and excel in them. Orion

Description

Music gives a soul to the universe, wings to the mind, flight to the imagination and life to everything.” Orion, the music club of IIT Goa, is a place where students come to jam, learn and make music of different kinds. It seeks to bring together everyone with a passion for any genre of music. Being one of the most active clubs, the club conducts productive music workshops and gives fierce competition to other artists.

The object of oratory alone in not truth, but persuasion.” Panache, the oratory club of IIT Goa, aims at enhancing the student’s ability to communicate their feelings by understanding their own emotions.   1. Celebration of Makar Sakranti and Lohri

    January 14th, 2020

    The cultural council organized bonfire, jam sessions, other fun games and kite flying events in lieu of makar sakranti and lohri.

  2. Tree plantation event

    January 17th, 2020

    A tree plantation event was conducted to begin with the festivities of CultRang 2020.

  3. Republic Day celebrations

    January 26th, 2020

    A cultural programme was organized by the council to mark the Republic Day.

  4. Blood donation camp

    January 31th, 2020

    A blood donation camp was organized under CultRang 2020.

  5. CultRang Marathon 2020

    February 7th, 2020

    A marathon was organized to inaugurate the event with fitness.

  6. CultRang 2020

    August 7th to 9th, 2020

    The college's first ever Techno Cultural fest was organized, which turned out to be a huge success.

  7. Gandhi Jayanti

    October 2nd, 2020

    The dance club, Meraki, and the music club, Orion, prepared a dance and a music video respectively for the celebration of Gandhi Jayanti on 2nd October 2020. A quiz competition was organised by the Oratory club, Panache, in the spirit of Gandhi Jayanti on 1st October 2020.

  8. National Education Day

    November 11th, 2020

    To mark the National Education Day on 11th November 2020, the council organised an Essay writing competition and a debate competition.

  • Diwali week

    November 11th to 18th, 2020

    The council conducted an Inter College Photography contest, Inter College Kavi Sammelan and an Intra College Decoration and Rangoli competitions over a week in the spirit of Diwali

  • Freshers Orientation program

    December 2020

    The introduction of the various cultural clubs and their members was given to the newly admitted first year students.

  • National Youth Day

    January 12th, 2020

    The literature club, Qalam, conducted an article writing competition to celebrate the National Youth Day marked every year on 12th June. Participants were required to share their perspectives on implementation of Swami Vivekananda’s values and principles in the modern era, highlighting their opinions on how the youth should reform their mentality and vision of the world.

  • Republic Day Celebration

    January 26th, 2021

    The dance club- Meraki, and the music club- Orion, prepared a dance and a music video respectively. The Fine Arts club, Eunoia, and the Literature club, Qalam, organised a poster making - quote writing competition for all the students of IIT Goa. The theme for the competition was “What does Republic mean to you?

  • Aaftaab- Literature

    February, 2021

    A number of literature competitions and fun activities were planned in the online mode.

  • CultRang 2021

    April 9th to 11th 2021

    The fest was organised completely online with various Cultural competitions, Fun events, Musical performances and a Comedy show.

  • 75th Independence Day celebration

    August 15th, 2021

    Orion(The music club) and Mukhota(The Drama club) prepared a music video and a drama video respectively.

  • Teacher’s Day celebration

    September 5th, 2021

    Originals along with Qalam released a poetic tribute video.

  • Ganeshotsav

    September 9th, 2021

    Meraki(Dance club) prepared a dance video on the occasion of Ganeshotsav.
