About Us

Driving Financial Awareness & Technology in Alpha

Alpha Club is a student-driven body of IIT Goa that aims to increase awareness about finance and economics within the community. We organise workshops, guest lectures, stock market simulations, and discussions to help students understand financial concepts, economic trends, and investment strategies.

Our activities also focus on exploring the intersection of technology and finance (FinTech, algo-trading, blockchain), encouraging students to view finance as a viable career option while leveraging their technical skills.

We believe in learning through case studies, simulations, and hands-on projects. Regular meetups and collaborative activities help members sharpen their analytical and practical finance skills.
