About Us

The Electronics and Robotics Club is a vibrant community of tech enthusiasts passionate about AI, IoT, robotics, and embedded systems. We innovate cutting-edge solutions, build autonomous robots, and explore futuristic technologies shaping tomorrow.

Our Vision :

  • Provide students—whether beginners or those with experience—with a collaborative, hands-on platform to learn electronics, mechanical design, and robotics.
  • Bridge the gap between theory and practice by organising workshops, events, and competitions on different topics.
  • Supporting student ideas and projects, helping them convert concepts into working prototypes, encouraging creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving.
