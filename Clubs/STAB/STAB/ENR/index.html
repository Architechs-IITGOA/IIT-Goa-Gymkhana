About Our Club

The Electronics & Robotics Club is a vibrant community for students passionate about technology, engineering, and innovation. We provide a platform for members to learn, experiment, and build exciting projects in electronics, programming, and robotics. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced enthusiast, you'll find a supportive environment to grow your skills and collaborate with like-minded individuals.

Our activities include:

  • Hands-on workshops and tutorials
  • Project development sessions
  • Participation in national and international competitions
  • Guest lectures and industry visits
  • Informal brainstorming and hacking sessions
