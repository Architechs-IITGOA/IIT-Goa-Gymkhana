About M-DASH

M-DASH club at IIT Goa aims to generate interest and impart knowledge among students, especially those with a mechanical engineering background. We run regular workshops and events to help students get comfortable with popular design software such as AutoCAD, SolidWorks, and more.

Lessons start from scratch — freshers are welcome. Our workshops cover fundamentals and practical, hands-on projects so members gain both knowledge and experience in design and manufacturing workflows.

Whether you're looking to prototype, design parts, or learn CAD tools from the ground up, M-DASH provides a supportive environment to build your skills.
