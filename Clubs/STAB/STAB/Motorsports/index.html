About Us

IIT Goa Motorsports is the Official Formula Student team of IIT Goa which develops prototype race cars and represent the Institute at national and international engineering design competitions like Formula Bharat, Formula Student Electric Vehicle (FSEV), FMEA-FKDC, GO Kart challenge, etc. The team gives you a platform to build exciting racing cars like formula one, go-karts, off-road vehicles, etc, and make a business plan for the cars built by the team. We aim to step towards Sustainable and carbon-neutral energy-powered innovations.
