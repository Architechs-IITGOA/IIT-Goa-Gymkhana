STAB-Student Technical Activities body, is the most vibrant body of IIT GOA, responsible to orgnize various Technical fests,and many more buzz inside or off campus.

About STAB

The most vibrant body of IIT Goa, STAB is responsible for organizing a variety of technical fests and events. It serves as the central hub for all technical activities on campus.

Technical Clubs

STAB oversees a wide range of specialized clubs, including ArchiTechs, GoMyno, InfoSec, MotorSports, and others, each fostering specific technical interests.

Key Events & Collaborations

STAB coordinates major events like the Smart India Internal Hackathon with IIT Goa's IIC and has partnered with organizations like Elite Techno Groups for internship workshops.
