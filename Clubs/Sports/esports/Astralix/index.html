Who are we?

Fueling Gaming Passion, Crafting Virtual Realities.

Step into Astralix, the beating heart of eSports at IIT Goa. We are the official gaming haven, where the excitement of Call of Duty, the strategy of Valorant, the intensity of BGMI, and the thrill of Clash Royale converge. As a vibrant eSports community, Astralix is not just about games; it's about forging connections and celebrating the spirit of competition. Join our online tournaments, where champions are made and unforgettable moments unfold. Astralix is your ticket to a dynamic world of gaming excellence, camaraderie, and endless excitement. Ready to dive into the action? Game on with Astralix – where gaming transcends pixels and becomes an extraordinary journey.

but we are family.
