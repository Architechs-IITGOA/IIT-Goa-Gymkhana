Athletics

Athletics is the base of all sports as it improves stamina and strength, which are the basic requirements for each and every sport. The college does not provide any particular facilities for athletics, but anyone who wishes to can practice running, skipping, exercising etc. on their own and the institute will try its best to cater to the needs of anyone who asks for it. IIT Goa has also shown participation in athletic events in Inter IIT Sports Meet and there is still scope to grow

Aquatics

Badminton, being the world's fastest racquet sport, requires flexibility, stamina and agility at its best. It is not only a game of great techniques and physical fitness, but also a game of tactics. With that as the central focus, IIT Goa provides vigorous training to the willing. Basketball

Basketball is a game of great perseverance, endurance and devotion. The same is highlighted in IIT Goa's culture of basketball. The dedicated team of the college can be seen practising regularly, making the best out of the facilities provided. The basketball court in the hostel campus is the highlight, with the three hostel blocks surrounding it, and it is very evident that basketball is the most played and enjoyed sport in the campus. Moreover, the location and accessibility of the court are noteworthy for the students who strive to work hard towards the intricacies of the sport.

Chess

The significance of chess goes way back in history as it is known as the oldest game of skill. Chess is all: science,art and sport in one. It exercises the brain and activates critical thinking skills. Such important skills are encouraged here and enthusiasts have overflowing opportunities with inter and intra college tournaments.

Cricket

The affinity our country has for the sport is already remarkable. Cricket is a thrill, both to play and to watch. The game encourages team spirit, fosters discipline, helps build up character and bring out leadership qualities. To keep the unique spirit of this particular sport alive even in college, we offer proper facilities of cricket training, with a newly made cricket pitch inside the hostel campus itself, and evenings dedicated to coaching sessions.

Football

Football is a physically demanding game that provides an opportunity for players to improve their speed, agility, strength, hand-eye coordination and overall cardiovascular endurance. Being a sport that requires proper training to compete, IIT Goa offers the facility of training enthusiasts to their best potential. With coaching sessions being conducted regularly early in the morning, its the best shot to improve your game.

With the provision of facilities like futsal nets and balls, the sport receives enthusiasm from both, the players and the spectators alike, especially during the tournaments.

Chakravyuh

Mr. Aryan Olkha, during his tenure as General Secretary of Sports Affairs in the academic year 2022-23, elevated campus sports through Chakravyuh - our college's flagship sports event. Chakravyuh is not just a sporting competition, but the pinnacle of our college's annual sports calendar. As the main sports event, it:

  1. Brings together students from across all departments.
  2. Showcases the most talented athletes on campus.
  3. Represents the pinnacle of inter-batch and inter-departmental sporting competition.
  4. Embodies the college's commitment to athletic excellence.
  5. Mr. Olkha's vision transformed Chakravyuh into more than an event - it became a celebration of athletic spirit and collegiate pride.

Inter-Block

A standalone futsal tournament featuring intense competition among hostel blocks, separate from the Chakravyuh sports event. The tournament focuses on:

  • Direct inter-block football competition.
  • Showcasing block-level sporting talent.
  • Creating campus-wide sporting engagement.

Aarambh

Aarambh is an annual sports event exclusively organized for first-year undergraduate students, offering a vibrant platform to connect with peers, foster camaraderie, and showcase athletic talent.

The event features an exciting lineup of sports, including box cricket, volleyball, basketball, table tennis, lawn tennis, chess, futsal, badminton, and athletics, ensuring something for everyone.

Beyond just competition, Aarambh emphasizes building bonds among freshers, helping them understand and collaborate with their fellow students.

For those who excel, the event serves as a gateway to representing the college in their respective sports, offering a chance to shine and contribute to the institution's teams.

Aarambh is more than just a tournamentâ€”it's a celebration of talent, teamwork, and new beginnings.

Cricverse

Cricverse emerged as a groundbreaking campus sports initiative, pioneered by Mr. Tarun Vasupally during his tenure as Outdoor Sports Secretary in 2022-23 and subsequently continued as General Secretary Sports Affairs in 2023-24. The event was further sustained by Mr. Bhardwaj Chakka in his role as General Secretary Sports Affairs in 2024-25.

The tournament's unique format centers around an exciting player auction system where teams strategically bid for players using virtual currency, creating an engaging pre-match experience.

Played as a box cricket event, Cricverse transforms traditional cricket by adapting to compact playing areas and introducing dynamic rules that emphasize skill and strategic team composition.

FICMST

As General Secretary Sports Affairs in 2024-25, Mr. Bharadwaj Chakka demonstrated exceptional leadership in transforming campus sports, with the First Inter-College Multi-Sports Tri-Series (FICMST) being a crowning achievement. His persistent efforts were instrumental in bringing NIT Goa and BITS Goa together for this landmark inter-collegiate sporting event.

Recognizing the potential for cross-institutional collaboration, Mr. Bharadwaj Chakka invested significant time and energy in negotiating, planning, and executing FICMST. He navigated administrative challenges, coordinated with different institutional sports departments, and created a comprehensive framework that enabled seamless participation from multiple colleges.

His vision extended beyond mere competition, aiming to create a platform that fosters athletic talent, builds inter-collegiate relationships, and provides students with broader sporting opportunities.

Through strategic planning and unwavering commitment, Mr. Bharadwaj Chakka transformed what could have been a routine sports event into a meaningful inter-institutional sporting experience.
